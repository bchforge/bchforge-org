Tools for the Bitcoin Cash ecosystem

BCH Forge

Open-source tools for Bitcoin Cash self-custody and infrastructure.

BCH Forge builds practical, auditable tools that help the Bitcoin Cash community build, run, and maintain useful systems without unnecessary complexity.
